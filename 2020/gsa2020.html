Student presentations at GSA 2020

Published: 2020-10-26
By Sean Mulcahy

In News.

Three students from the Western Mars Lab are presenting virtual posters at GSA 2020. Please stop and say hello if you’re attending the conference this year!

  • Poster 14-13: Finlay, O.F., Kraft, M.D., Rice, M.S., Hoza, K.M., and Mulcahy, S.R., 2020, SEM characterization of naturally weathered basalt surfaces for understanding photometry of weathering rinds, GSA Annual Meeting, doi: 1130/abs/2020AM-359292.
  • Poster 14-14: Eng, A.M., Rice, M., Kraft, M.D., Lapo, K., Hoza, K.M., and Mulcahy, S.R., 2020, Influence of silica coatings on the reflectance spectra of mars analog basalts, GSA Annual Meeting, doi: 1130/abs/2020AM-359885.
  • Poster 14-15: Seppi, I.R., Rice, M., Kraft, M.D., Lapo, K., Mulcahy, S.R., and Hoza, K.M., 2020, Spectral variability of weathered basalts and sandstones, GSA Annual Meeting, doi:1130/abs/2020AM-359979.
