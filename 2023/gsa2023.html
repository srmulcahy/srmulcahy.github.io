2023 GSA Cordilleran Meeting Presentations

Published: 2023-05-20
By Sean Mulcahy

In News.

tags: students publications

Our research group presented a series of talks and posters at the recent 2023 GSA Cordilleran meeting in Reno, NV. The presentations centered on our work to understand the record of Jurassic-Cretaceous subduction zone metamorphism and deformation preserved in the Northwest Cascades. Use the links below to see the text of our abstracts.

  • Cunetta, N., Schermer, E.L., and Mulcahy, S.R., 2023, Structural evolution of subduction initiation and early cooling: insights from the Easton metamorphic suite, northwest Washington, GSA Abstracts with Programs, v. 55, doi: 10.1130/abs/2023CD-387478.
  • Gates, E., Mulcahy, S.R., and Schermer, E.L., 2023, Evidence for accretion and underplating of distinct units within regional blueschists of the Easton metamorphic suite, northwest Cascades Washington, GSA Abstracts with Programs, v. 55, doi: 10.1130/abs/2023CD-387477.
  • Mulcahy, S.R., Schermer, E.L., *Cordova, J., Cunetta, N., and Gates, E., 2023, Evidence and timing for multiple cooling mechanisms in a long-lived subduction zone from the Easton metamorphic suite in the northwest Cascades, GSA Abstracts with Programs, v. 55, doi: 10.1130/abs/2023CD-387367.
